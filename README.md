# ◉ One Watch

> **Eliminate choice paralysis. One perfect pick — or the full catalogue. Your call.**

One Watch is a streaming intelligence concept that unifies major streaming platforms into a single, unbiased recommendation surface. Originally built at a hackathon, the project was later expanded independently to explore the interaction design and UX of the core idea more deeply.

---

## Background

One Watch started as a hackathon project — a team effort to go from a blank screen to a working prototype in a matter of hours. After the hackathon, the concept was taken further independently: refining the UI, building out the explore experience, and pushing the interaction design to better reflect the original vision.

---

## The Problem

Every evening, millions of people sit down to unwind — only to hit a wall of digital friction. With great content scattered across half a dozen apps, finding something to watch has become a chore. Biased promotional algorithms, fragmented catalogues, and endless scrolling eat into the time people actually wanted to spend relaxing.

One Watch treats all streaming platforms as a single ecosystem and gets you to a decision in under 60 seconds.

---

## Two Modes

### 🎯 Focus Mode
Answer a few questions — who's watching, what's the mood, what format — and the app returns a curated shortlist matched to your intent. No scrolling. No filters. One decision.

### 🌐 Explore Mode
Browse an aggregated catalogue filtered by genre. A horizontal carousel surfaces titles with full poster art and logos. Tap any card for a full title breakdown with reviews, cast, and collections.
